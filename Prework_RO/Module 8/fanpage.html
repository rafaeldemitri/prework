Background

Larry June is an upcoming American rapper born on April 8,1991. He is originally from San Francisco, California, but he was raised in Atlanta, Georgia. Larry started to stand out in the rap game because of his obnoxious ad-libs along with his aggressive, yet witty, lyrics. His universal logo is an orange fruit, which he has started to merchandise on clothing. With the help of his music, Larry has done collaborations with Colt-45 Malt Liquor, Whole Foods, and other big named brands. He is actively signed to Warner Bros. Records and is persistent with releasing new music.
